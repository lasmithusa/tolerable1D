About

Tolerable1D is a tool for running one-dimensional tolerance analysis on mechanical parts and assemblies using the Monte Carlo method. Tolerable1D allows you to quickly predict the expected variation of your designs at production volumes.

Why this tool was created? Excel plugins got me down. I needed a pain free way to run Monte Carlo simulations when designing press fits, snap tabs, and seals in my work as an R&D Engineer. This tool fit the bill. I'd like this tool to improve your workflow as it has mine.
